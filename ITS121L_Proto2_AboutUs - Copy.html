ABOUT PEKAMURA DINER

About Us:

At Pekamura Diner, we are passionate about creating memorable dining experiences for our guests. From our carefully crafted menu to our warm and inviting atmosphere, we strive to make every visit special. Our journey began with a vision to provide not just a meal, but a culinary journey that tantalizes your taste buds and leaves you wanting more. Explore our diverse menu, carefully curated by our talented chefs, offering a fusion of flavors that reflect our commitment to quality and innovation.



How We Started:

We started our business by running a sushi shop called Chay’s Sushi Bar before innovating our restaurant to Pekamura Diner. At Chay’s Sushi Bar, we sell Japanese delicacies such as sushi packs, sashimi, and other Japanese-related foods. Our desire to serve more than just Japanese food made the idea of creating Pekamura Diner come to life.
